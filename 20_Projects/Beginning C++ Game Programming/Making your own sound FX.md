Sound effects (FX) can be downloaded for free from sites such as Freesound (www.freesound.org) but, often, the license won’t allow you to use them if you are selling your game. 
Another option is to use an open-source software called BFXR from www.bfxr.net, which can help you generate lots of different sound FX that are yours to keep and do with as you like.
