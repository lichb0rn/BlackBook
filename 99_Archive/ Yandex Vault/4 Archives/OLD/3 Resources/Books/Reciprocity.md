# [[Reciprocity]]

Reciprocity teaches us why win-win relationships are the way to go, why waiters leave candies with the bill, why it‚Äôs a good idea to use the least force possible to secure an outcome, and why a lot of companies don‚Äôt permit their employees to accept gifts.

In the natural world, this third law of [[Isaac Newton|Newton]]‚Äôs explains jet propulsion. Reciprocity can be summed up like this: **when you act on things, they act on you.**

## The rise of the "win-win"
