# [[Black Swan problem]]

Black Swans (capitalized) are large-scale [[Predictability|unpredictable]] and irregular events of massive consequence—unpredicted by a certain observer, and such unpredictor is generally called the “turkey” when he is both surprised and harmed by these event.
