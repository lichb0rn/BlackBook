# [[The six-level model for reviewing your own work]]

- Horizon 5: Life
- Horizon 4: Long-term visions
- Horizon 3: One-to two-year goals
- Horizon 2: Areas of focus and accountability
- Horizon 1: Current projects
- Ground: Current actions
